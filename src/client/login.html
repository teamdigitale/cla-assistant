We appreciate community contributions to code repositories open sourced by Presidency Italian of the Council of Ministers, Digital Transformation Department. By signing a contributor license agreement, we ensure that the community is free to use your contributions.

Sign the CLA
When you contribute to a Presidency of the Council of Ministers, Digital Transformation Department open source project on GitHub with a new pull request, a bot will evaluate whether you have signed the CLA. If required, the bot will comment on the pull request, including a link to this system to accept the agreement.
